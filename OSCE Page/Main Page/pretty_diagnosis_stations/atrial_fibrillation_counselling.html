High-Yield OSCE Points 🧠

Set up

  • 🧼 Wash hands and don PPE if appropriate
  • 👋 Introduce yourself including name and role
  • 📇 Confirm patient’s name and date of birth
  • 📋 Explain purpose: counselling on atrial fibrillation
  • ✔️ Gain consent to proceed
  • 📚 Be aware of key background info like recent AF diagnosis

Perceptions

  • 🔍 Explore patient’s prior knowledge/perception of AF
  • ❓ Explore concerns and expectations
  • 🙋 Encourage patient questions throughout

Invitation

  • 💬 Offer more info about AF
  • ❓ Ask if patient has specific questions
  • ✅ Check which areas patient wants to know more about
  • 👥 Confirm patient wants info now and if anyone else should be present

Knowledge

  • 🫀 Explain AF (irregular heartbeat caused by abnormal atrial electrical activity)
  • ⚠️ Describe causes, including hypertension
  • 🩺 Explain management: rate vs rhythm control
  • 💊 Explain anticoagulation to reduce stroke risk
  • 📦 Deliver info in chunks and pause to check understanding
  • 🗣 Avoid jargon and use patient-friendly terms
  • 🔄 Tailor depth of info based on patient knowledge/preferences
  • ✔️ Correct misunderstandings as they arise
  • 📲 Provide additional resources like websites and leaflets

Exploration & Empathy

  • ❤️ Explore how patient feels about the information given
  • 🤝 Show empathy throughout

Strategy & Summary

  • 🔄 Check patient’s understanding by asking them to summarise key points
  • ✔️ Confirm all questions/needs addressed
  • 🤝 Use shared decision-making to agree on plan
  • ⚠️ Provide safety-netting advice
