üìö Knowledge

  • A blood transfusion replaces lost components of blood, mainly red cells, due to blood loss, anaemia, or surgery.
  • Reduces symptoms like breathlessness and fatigue caused by anaemia.
  • Increases oxygen-carrying capacity and can improve post-op recovery.
  • Very low risk of infections due to rigorous screening (<1 in a million).
  • Minor risks: fever, chills, rash; rare risks: mismatch, overload, infections.
  • Alternatives include iron therapy, erythropoietin, and cell salvage.
